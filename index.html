How to Play:

  • Click the "Start" button to begin game.
  • Simon will provide a sequence of colors.
  • Click on the color buttons to repeat the exact sequence Simon just played.

Winning:

  • If you successfully complete the pattern up to the maximum level of 15, you win the game!

Losing:

  • If you make an incorrect input, you'll receive a game over message.

Restart

  • At any time, you can click the "RESTART" button to start a new game.
