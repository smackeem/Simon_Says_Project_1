How to Play:

  1. Click "Start" button to begin.
  2. Simon will provide a sequence of colors.
  3. Click on colored buttons to repeat the exact sequence.
  4. If you successfully complete the patterns up to the maximum level of 15, you win the game!
  5. If you make an incorrect input, you'll receive a game over message.
  6. At any time, you can click the "RESTART" button to start a new game.
